# Call for Speakers  

**Date:** 2–3 July 2026  
**Location:** Technical University of Munich (TUM), Freising Campus, Munich, Germany  

We invite researchers, industry practitioners, and innovators to submit abstracts for talks at the **WHISKEY-AI Workshop 2026**, a multidisciplinary forum exploring how **physics-informed edge AI** can transform **food fermentation and beverage ageing processes** while reducing environmental impact.

This workshop aims to bring together expertise from **artificial intelligence, process engineering, food science, chemistry, and sustainability** to accelerate innovation in fermentation-driven industries such as brewing, distilling, dairy, and alternative proteins.

## Topics of Interest

We welcome contributions including (but not limited to):

- Physics-informed machine learning for fermentation and ageing processes  
- Edge AI and real-time monitoring in food and beverage production  
- Digital twins and hybrid modeling of biochemical processes  
- Sensor technologies and embedded intelligence for process control  
- Sustainable and energy-efficient fermentation and maturation techniques  
- Accelerated ageing methodologies for beverages (e.g., whiskey, wine, beer)  
- Data-driven optimization of flavor, quality, and yield  
- Case studies from industry applications  
- Lifecycle assessment and environmental impact reduction
